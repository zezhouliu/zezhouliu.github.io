About Me

Zezhou (Alex) Liu

Harvard 2016


My name is Alex Liu and I'm a junior at Harvard pursuing an A.B. in Computer Science and a secondary in Economics. I'm passionate about Big Data, software engineering, and systems programming!

My software engineering experience includes internships with Redbeacon and Microsoft. At Redbeacon I worked on the mobile team to develop an app for iOS6 + 7. At Microsoft I worked on developing an intelligent data analytics tool for Outlook to analyze email behavior and suggest better email practices.

I'm very interested in database research and am a part of the DAS Research Lab led by Professor Stratos Idreos. My current project focuses on improving join performances by exploiting fast-scans, compression techniques, and the high-degree of parallelism of column-store databases.
