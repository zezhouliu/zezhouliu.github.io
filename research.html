Research Interests

Database Research @ DAS Lab

Undergraduate Researcher


My current research interests are in the area of databases and systems architecture and design. I am an undergraduate researcher for Professor Stratos Idreos as part of the DAS Research Lab. My current project focuses on improving join performances by exploiting the fast-scans, compression techniques, and high-degree of parallelism of column-store databases.
